WEAPONS AND RIFLES

The release date may be a mystery for now, but we do have some
information on who Diego exactly is. He is the son of a fascist leader
called Antón Castillo who is the leader of an isolationist country that's
inspired by Cuba. Just like his father, Diego will be an antagonist
in the game as you try to save the island of Yara from their tyranny.
It will be an intriguing character piece as it may tackle how this
child's mind is warped as a revolt sparks across the country.
